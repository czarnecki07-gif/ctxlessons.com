What is CTXLESSONS?

A calm reading space for interactive CTXBOOKs.

CTXLESSONS is not a generator. It is the public library where readers discover, open and read CTXBOOK publications.

Interactive reading

Text, media and CTX explanations stay inside the publication.

CTX explanations

Selected terms can be explained without leaving the reader.

Collections

Authors
